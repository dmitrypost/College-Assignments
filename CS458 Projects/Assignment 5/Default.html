The “Dodge the Fireworks” Extravaganza
Evansville to Las Vegas 7/4/2013
Leaves 6:00am 7/4/2013
Returns 6:00am 7/6/2013
First Class: $727.25 per adult
Coach Class: $535.00 per adult
Standing Room-Only Class: Not Available

Evansville, Indiana residents, Scott and René Anderson, wish to announce the Summer 2013 creation of Anderson Zeppelin Company (AZC).  AZC will operate a fleet of six zeppelins headquartered at Dress Regional Airport in Evansville. Regularly scheduled flights will be offered between Evansville and St. Louis, Chicago, Indianapolis, Nashville, Pittsburgh and Atlanta. The zeppelins are all brand-new Boehne BZ-17 models which offer the ultimate in zeppelin travel.  Three levels of service are offered with the BZ-17: first class, coach class and standing room-only class.  First class includes reclining Recaro seating, unlimited complementary drinks and appetizers.  Coach class includes wooden bench seating and unlimited tap water.  Standing room-only class offers a 2 foot by 2 foot floor area for standing.  Due to the use of hydrogen for buoyancy, smoking is not permitted.   Scott and René Anderson own Anderson Zeppelin Company, Boehne Dirigible Works, Air Rescue Associates, Discount Blimp Pilot Licensing, Inc. and Hydrogen Manufacturing Gmbh.
